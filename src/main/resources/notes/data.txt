Data:

character:
- name
- items (use slots?)
- spells known
- stats
- race (modifies stats)
- controller (player id / computer)

stats:
- health (max and current)
- mana (max and current)
- physical resist (from armor)
- magic resist (from armor)
- strength (hit modifier)
- dexterity (accuracy)
- agility (move speed)
- constitution (max hp)
- focus (needed for higher level spells)
- will (max mp)

arena:
- positions of characters
- positions of scenery
- size of map

weapon:
- modifiers
- range
- weight?

spell:
- effect

armor:
- modifiers
- weight?

items:
- effect
- weight?
